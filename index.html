The Rules of Quattro

1. The computer picks a random 4-letter word.

2. You must now come up with a valid 4-letter word that differs by exactly one letter. For example, if the computer's word is "PLAY", you might submit "PLOY" or "CLAY". No repetitions!

3. The computer responds under the same rules.

4. Each word is scored - Scrabble style - and a running tally is kept.

5. The game ends when either you are ahead by 5 or more points or the computer is ahead by 10 or more points.

6. Have fun!
